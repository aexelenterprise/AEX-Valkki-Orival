Phonotactics & Stress

Syllable structure: (C)(C)V(C)
Allowed onset clusters: fl, vr, kr, st, wl, wr, tr, etc.
Stress: usually penultimate
Glottal stop ʔ: inserted before vowel-initial words in careful speech (especially formal Örival-Standard).
